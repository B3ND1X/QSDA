Ingredients:
- Pulled Beef: 1.135 kg
- Chipotle Paste: 1.5 tbls
- Cumin: 1 tbls
- Cloves: 1/2 tsp
- Vinegar: 3 tbls
- Water: 1/4 cup

Procedure:
1. Mix chipotle paste, cumin, and cloves.
2. Add vinegar and water slowly to form marinade.
3. Shred thawed pulled beef and remove any fat or tendons.
4. Mix beef with marinade until fully coated.
5. Store in 1/3 stainless container and label.
6. Reheat in Rational (500g, 4.5 mins, '1 kg meat' setting) or TurboChef (flatten package, select '2x500g reheat').
7. Ensure internal temperature reaches 74Â°C.

Ingredients:
- White Vinegar: 1 litre (4 cups)
- Salt: 3 tbls
- White Sugar: 1/2 cup

Procedure:
1. In a 4 QT container, combine vinegar, sugar, and salt. Stir until dissolved.
2. Cut ends off onions, peel, and slice into 3/16" pieces using slicer.

4. Ensure onions are fully submerged, cover, and refrigerate.
5. Marinate for 24 hours before serving.
